A GUIDE TO SPREADING ASHES

SPREADING ASH AT SEA

Federal law regulates the spreading of ashes at sea:

  • You must be 3 nautical miles from the shore before scattering the ashes at sea. The depth of the water doesnâ€™t matter.
  • Only decomposable items can be scattered on the water. This includes scattering urns, wreaths, etc.
  • After scattering ashes at sea, you should notify the EPA within 30 days of burial.

For More Information:

https://www.epa.gov/ocean-dumping/burial-sea
